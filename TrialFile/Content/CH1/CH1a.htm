Data Primitives, Variables, and Math

It's odd, in a way, to begin a book on Java by writing about data primitives, since Java is so strongly object-oriented. My Java Mentor, Maxim, has a positive aversion to numbers -- and he is a mathematician and physicist. He claims to have had little to do with numbers since the eighth grade. It's not that he has anything against number per se; he just resents them because they aren't objects. Still, we need them. Even Maxim concedes that, though he eyes any number with suspicion.
